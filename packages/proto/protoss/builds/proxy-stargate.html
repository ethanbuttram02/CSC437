Proxy Stargate

Matchup: All matchups

Difficulty: Intermediate

An aggressive build that places a Stargate close to the opponent's base for fast air unit harassment. This strategy relies on surprise and can deal significant economic damage or end games quickly.

Build Order

  1. 9 Pylon
  2. 13 Gateway
  3. 15 Assimilator
  4. 16 Pylon
  5. 18 Cybernetics Core
  6. 19 Zealot
  7. 20 Assimilator
  8. @Cyber: Warpgate Research
  9. 22 Probe (for proxy)
  10. 24 Stargate (proxy location)
  11. @Stargate: Phoenix or Void Ray
  12. Continue air unit production
  13. Add Gateway units for support

Unit Choice and Usage

Phoenix

Best vs: Terran, Protoss

Advantages

  • Fast, mobile, good vs light units
  • Graviton Beam for lifting units
  • Hit and run tactics

Targets

  • Workers, Marines, Stalkers
  • Lift key units like Tanks or Queens

Void Ray

Best vs: Zerg, Protoss

Advantages

  • Strong vs buildings and heavy units
  • Charge beam for increased damage
  • Good staying power

Targets

  • Buildings, heavy units
  • Focus firing for beam management

Oracle

Best vs: All matchups

Advantages

  • Worker harassment specialist
  • Revelation for scouting
  • Fast and elusive

Targets

  • Workers, key tech buildings
  • Pulsar Beam harassment

Matchup Specific Strategy

vs Terran

Strategy

  • Phoenix preferred unit choice
  • Target SCVs, Marines, Medivacs
  • Hit before significant Marine count

Watch For

  • Marines, Turrets, Vikings
  • Follow-up: Gateway units or expand

vs Protoss

Strategy

  • Phoenix or Void Ray work well
  • Target Probes, Pylons, tech buildings
  • Hit before Stalker mass or air response

Watch For

  • Stalkers, Photon Cannons
  • Follow-up: Tech transition or all-in

vs Zerg

Strategy

  • Void Ray or Oracle preferred
  • Target Drones, Queens, Overlords
  • Hit before Spire or Hydra Den

Watch For

  • Queens, Spore Crawlers, Hydras
  • Follow-up: Pressure or macro behind

Proxy Locations

Ideal Positions

  • Dead Space: Areas opponent rarely scouts
  • Behind Minerals: Hidden from main base vision
  • High Ground: Harder to spot from below
  • Corner Maps: Edges of the map
  • Near Natural: Close to secondary target

What to Avoid

  • Obvious scouting paths
  • Areas with enemy unit patrol
  • Too close to enemy anti-air
  • Locations with no escape route
  • Areas visible from main base

Common Mistakes

  • Proxy Stargate spotted by enemy scout
  • Poor proxy location choice
  • Not bringing enough air units
  • Attacking into prepared anti-air
  • Losing the proxy Probe too early
  • No follow-up plan if harassment fails
  • Over-committing against heavy defense
  • Not scouting for enemy tech responses

Advanced Techniques

  • Double Proxy: Two Stargates for more units
  • Fake Retreat: Bait out anti-air, then re-engage
  • Multi-angle Attack: Hit from different directions
  • Probe Micro: Use proxy Probe for scouting
  • Pylon Power: Build Pylons to power proxy

Transition Options

If Harassment Succeeds

  • Continue air unit production
  • Add more Stargates
  • Expand behind the pressure
  • Transition to normal tech

If Harassment Fails

  • Recall air units to defend
  • Focus on gateway units
  • Defensive play until caught up
  • Consider different tech path

Protoss Build Orders

Channel the power of the ancient Protoss with their advanced technology and powerful units. Master timing attacks and late-game compositions with these sophisticated strategies.

Core Protoss Builds

  • 4-Gate Rush
  • 2-Base Colossus
  • PvZ Forge Fast Expand
  • Proxy Stargate
  • Dark Templar Rush
  • Carrier Rush

Build Overviews

4-Gate Rush Beginner

Matchup: All matchups (especially PvP) | Best For: Learning warpgate mechanics

Advantages

  • Simple and reliable execution
  • Strong early-game pressure
  • Teaches warpgate mechanics
  • Can end games quickly
  • Good vs greedy economic builds

Disadvantages

  • Easily countered with proper scouting
  • All-in nature - hard to recover
  • Weak late-game potential
  • Predictable at higher levels
  • Requires continuous unit production

2-Base Colossus Intermediate

Matchup: PvT, PvZ | Best For: Mid-game timing attacks

Advantages

  • Powerful mid-game timing attack
  • Excellent vs ground armies
  • Good range and splash damage
  • Strong vs bio compositions
  • Scales well with upgrades

Disadvantages

  • Vulnerable to air units (Vikings, Corruptors)
  • Expensive tech investment
  • Slow unit movement
  • Can be outmaneuvered
  • Requires good positioning

PvZ Forge Fast Expand Advanced

Matchup: Protoss vs Zerg | Best For: Economic advantage

Advantages

  • Strong economic foundation
  • Safe vs most Zerg openings
  • Early upgrades (+1 attack)
  • Good vs Zergling rushes
  • Excellent late-game potential

Disadvantages

  • Vulnerable to air harassment
  • Requires precise execution
  • Delayed military units
  • Can be overwhelmed by timing attacks
  • High skill requirement

Proxy Stargate Intermediate

Matchup: All matchups | Best For: Surprise harassment

Advantages

  • Fast air units for harassment
  • Surprise factor
  • Good vs opponents with no anti-air
  • Can transition to macro if successful
  • Strong worker harassment

Disadvantages

  • Can be scouted and countered
  • Risky investment if proxy found
  • Vulnerable to early pressure
  • Limited ground army early
  • Requires good probe micro for proxy

Dark Templar Rush Intermediate

Matchup: All matchups | Best For: Cheese strategy

Advantages

  • Can win instantly vs no detection
  • Invisible units provide surprise
  • Excellent harassment potential
  • Forces opponent to invest in detection
  • Good psychological pressure

Disadvantages

  • Completely shut down by detection
  • Expensive tech investment
  • Weak vs prepared opponents
  • Limited army if rush fails
  • One-dimensional strategy

Carrier Rush Advanced

Matchup: PvT, PvZ | Best For: Late-game dominance

Advantages

  • Extremely powerful late-game units
  • Long range and high damage
  • Good vs most ground armies
  • Interceptors provide micro advantage
  • Strong vs players who can't counter air

Disadvantages

  • Very expensive and slow to build
  • Vulnerable during tech-up phase
  • Countered by mass anti-air
  • Requires excellent defense early
  • Can be rushed down before completion

General Protoss Strategy Tips

  • Chronoboost: Use on key upgrades, units, and worker production
  • Force Fields: Master Sentry micro to split enemy armies
  • Positioning: Keep fragile units like Colossi protected
  • Tech Paths: Adapt your composition based on opponent's strategy
  • Harassment: Use Warp Prisms for multi-pronged attacks
  • Upgrades: Protoss units benefit greatly from weapon/armor upgrades

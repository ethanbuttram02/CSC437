ZvP Roach Rush

Matchup: Zerg vs Protoss

Difficulty: Beginner

An aggressive mid-game timing that hits before Protoss can establish strong gateway units with upgrades. This build excels at overwhelming gateway armies with tanky Roaches.

Build Order

  1. 9 Overlord
  2. 15 Hatchery
  3. 16 Spawning Pool
  4. 17 Extractor
  5. 18 Overlord
  6. @Pool: Queen, 2 Zerglings
  7. 22 Roach Warren
  8. 24 Overlord
  9. @Natural: Queen
  10. @Warren: Mass Roach production
  11. 28 Overlord
  12. Attack with 8-10 Roaches
  13. Continue Roach reinforcement

Execution Guide

Early Game (0-5 minutes)

  • Standard economic opening with expansion
  • Get Queens for defense and larvae inject
  • Scout Protoss build with Overlord
  • Build Roach Warren after basic defense
  • Focus on drone production until Warren completes

Production Phase (5-7 minutes)

  • Mass produce Roaches from both Hatcheries
  • Stop drone production temporarily
  • Build Overlords to avoid supply blocks
  • Continue larvae injecting
  • Scout Protoss army composition

Attack Phase (7-9 minutes)

  • Attack with 8-10 Roaches
  • Target gateway units and buildings
  • Continuously reinforce with more Roaches
  • Focus fire on key units (Stalkers first)
  • Don't over-commit if heavily defended

Key Strategic Points

  • Timing: Hit before Protoss gets Immortals/Colossi
  • Mass: Need critical mass of Roaches (8+ minimum)
  • Focus Fire: Target Stalkers and key units
  • Reinforcement: Keep producing during attack
  • Retreat Timing: Don't lose entire army

Roach Usage and Micro

Target Priority

Primary Targets

  • Stalkers (main threat to Roaches)
  • Sentries (prevent Force Field usage)
  • Zealots (after ranged units)
  • Buildings (Pylons and production)

Micro Techniques

  • Focus fire all Roaches on single target
  • Spread to avoid Force Field splits
  • Engage at close range
  • Pull back damaged Roaches

Scouting Information

What to Look For

Intelligence Gathering

  • Gateway count and unit numbers
  • Tech buildings (Robotics, Stargate)
  • Expansion timing
  • Unit composition ratios

Ideal Targets

  • Fast expansion builds
  • Tech-heavy strategies
  • Gateway-only compositions
  • Players with poor Force Field usage

Avoid Against

  • Early Immortal timing: Hard counter to Roaches
  • Heavy Cannon defense: Static defense too strong
  • 4-Gate all-ins: Too many units early
  • Early air harassment: Can disrupt economy

Dealing with Protoss Responses

vs Gateway Units

Unit Counters

  • Zealots: Easy target, kite with Roaches
  • Stalkers: Focus fire, avoid blink micro
  • Sentries: Kill first to prevent Force Fields
  • Mixed Army: Target ranged units first

vs Force Fields

  • Spread army to avoid splits
  • Target Sentries immediately
  • Wait out Force Fields (temporary)
  • Attack from multiple angles

vs Static Defense

  • Photon Cannons: Attack from range, focus fire
  • Pylon Power: Target Pylons powering Cannons
  • Different Angle: Find undefended approach
  • Economic Damage: Hit expansions instead

Economic Management

Drone Production

Production Phases

  • Early Game: Drone to ~28-30 workers
  • Attack Phase: Stop droning, make Roaches
  • Behind Attack: Resume droning if safe
  • Recovery: Drone up after attack resolves

Gas Usage

  • Roach Warren: 150 gas investment
  • Roaches: 25 gas each
  • Speed upgrade: Optional (100/100)
  • Saturation: 3 drones on gas

Common Mistakes

  • Attacking with too few Roaches (less than 8)
  • Not focusing fire on key units
  • Getting split by Force Fields
  • Over-committing against heavy defense
  • Not reinforcing during attack
  • Fighting at maximum Stalker range
  • Forgetting to inject larvae during attack
  • Poor target prioritization

Upgrade Considerations

Priority Upgrades

Essential Upgrades

  • Roach Speed: Tunneling Claws (100/100)
  • +1 Attack: Missile upgrades
  • +1 Carapace: Survivability boost

When to Upgrade

  • Roach Speed: If planning extended play
  • Attack Upgrades: Behind successful attack
  • During Attack: Focus on units first

Transition Options

If Attack Succeeds

Follow-up Options

  • Continue Roach pressure
  • Take third base
  • Add Hydralisk Den for range
  • Tech to Lair for advanced units

If Attack Fails

  • Retreat remaining Roaches
  • Focus on economy and defense
  • Tech to different composition
  • Prepare for Protoss counter-attack

Advanced Techniques

  • Proxy Roach Warren: Build closer to Protoss base
  • Multi-pronged Attack: Hit multiple locations
  • Roach Drops: Use Overlords for transport
  • Fake Retreat: Bait Protoss forward
  • Economic Harassment: Target expansions

Good Maps

Favorable Terrain

  • Open maps with direct attack paths
  • Wide chokes (hard to Force Field)
  • Short rush distances
  • Multiple attack angles

Difficult Maps

  • Maps with narrow chokes
  • High ground defensive positions
  • Long rush distances
  • Easy Force Field positions

Defending Against Roach Rush

Protoss Counter-Strategies

  • Early Immortals: Hard counter to Roaches
  • Force Fields: Split Roach army effectively
  • Cannons: Static defense at choke points
  • Unit Count: Have enough gateway units
  • High Ground: Defensive positioning advantage

Practice Drills

  • Practice build order timing execution
  • Work on Roach focus fire micro
  • Practice dealing with Force Fields
  • Learn optimal attack timing
  • Practice transitioning after attack
  • Work on larvae inject timing during battles

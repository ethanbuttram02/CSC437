ZvT Macro Hatch

Matchup: Zerg vs Terran

Difficulty: Intermediate

A solid economic build that prioritizes drone production and map control while staying safe against Terran pressure. This build focuses on out-macroing Terran players and transitioning to powerful late-game compositions.

Build Order

  1. 9 Overlord
  2. 15 Hatchery
  3. 16 Spawning Pool
  4. 17 Extractor
  5. 18 Overlord
  6. @Pool: 2 Zerglings, Queen
  7. 22 Overlord
  8. @Natural: Queen
  9. 24 2 Zerglings
  10. 26 Metabolic Boost (Speed)
  11. 28 Baneling Nest
  12. 30 Lair
  13. 32 Extractor x2
  14. @Lair: Spire or Hydralisk Den
  15. Continue based on Terran's composition

Execution Guide

Early Game (0-6 minutes)

  • Take fast expansion with Hatchery first
  • Get Pool and gas for basic defense
  • Build minimal Zerglings for scouting
  • Focus heavily on drone production
  • Get Queens for inject and defense
  • Scout Terran's opening build

Mid Game (6-12 minutes)

  • Research Metabolic Boost for Zergling speed
  • Add Baneling Nest for defense/pressure options
  • Drone up to 2-base saturation (44 drones)
  • Begin Lair tech for advanced units
  • Spread creep with Queens aggressively
  • Scout heavily for Terran timing attacks

Late Game Transition (12+ minutes)

  • Take third base when safe
  • Tech to Spire or Hydralisk Den based on scouting
  • Build army composition to counter Terran
  • Apply pressure with mobile units
  • Control map with creep spread and vision
  • Transition to Hive tech for endgame

Key Strategic Points

  • Economic Focus: Out-drone Terran early game
  • Inject Timing: Never miss Queen injects
  • Creep Spread: Map control and unit speed
  • Scouting: Adapt to Terran's strategy
  • Mobility: Use Zerg's speed advantage
  • Adaptation: Counter Terran's unit choices

Queen Management

Inject Larvae

Injection Fundamentals

  • Never let inject energy go to waste
  • Inject every 40 seconds precisely
  • Use base camera hotkeys for efficiency
  • One Queen per Hatchery minimum

Creep Spread

  • Begin spreading at 4-5 minutes
  • Connect main and natural first
  • Spread toward Terran base
  • Cover likely attack paths

Defense and Utility

  • Anti-Air: Queens counter Medivacs and air units
  • Transfuse: Heal important units and structures
  • Positioning: Keep Queens safe but accessible
  • Support: Use for defense against drops

Scouting and Information

What to Scout For

Opening Builds

  • 1-Rax expand, 3-Rax, or Reaper expand
  • Army composition (Bio, Mech, or Sky)
  • Timing attacks and tech choices
  • Expansion timing patterns

Scouting Methods

  • 9 Overlord scout for opening info
  • Active Zergling scouting and runbys
  • Overlord positioning for army movement
  • Sacrificial Overlords for information

Defending Against Terran Aggression

Reaper Harassment (3-5 minutes)

  • Response: 1-2 Queens, keep drones mining
  • Positioning: Queens near mineral lines
  • Don't Overreact: Continue drone production
  • Spine Crawlers: Only if heavy pressure

Marine/Marauder Timing (6-8 minutes)

  • Units: Speedlings and Banelings
  • Positioning: Fight on creep for speed advantage
  • Micro: Surround with Zerglings, detonate Banelings
  • Support: Queen transfuse and anti-air
  • Spine Crawlers: Add if overwhelmed

Tank Pushes (8-12 minutes)

  • Units: Mutalisks or Roaches for mobility
  • Strategy: Don't engage Tank lines directly
  • Flanking: Attack from multiple angles
  • Harassment: Hit Terran's economy simultaneously
  • Patience: Wait for good engagement opportunities

Tech Transitions

vs Bio (Marine/Marauder/Medivac)

Core Strategy

  • Core Composition: Ling/Bane/Muta
  • Tech Path: Baneling Nest → Lair → Spire
  • Strategy: Mobility and multi-pronged attacks
  • Late Game: Add Ultralisks or Brood Lords

vs Mech (Tank/Hellbat/Thor)

  • Core Composition: Roach/Hydra/Viper
  • Tech Path: Roach Warren → Lair → Hydra Den
  • Strategy: Range and Viper support
  • Late Game: Brood Lords with Corruptor support

vs Sky Terran (Battlecruiser/Viking)

  • Core Composition: Hydra/Corruptor
  • Tech Path: Hydra Den → Greater Spire
  • Strategy: Heavy anti-air focus
  • Support: Spore Crawlers at bases
  • Late Game: Viper support with Parasitic Bomb

Economic Management

Drone Production Priorities

Economic Targets

  • Constant drone production to 44 drones
  • 16 drones per mineral line saturation
  • 3 per geyser when needed for tech
  • Stop droning when large attack incoming

Base Management

  • Natural expansion at 15 supply
  • Third base at 8-10 minutes if safe
  • Fourth base at 12-15 minutes in macro games
  • Creep connection between all bases

Army Compositions by Game Stage

Early Defense (6-10 minutes)

Core Units

  • Zerglings and Banelings
  • 3:1 Zergling to Baneling vs Bio
  • Queens for inject, defense, creep
  • Fight on creep when possible

Mid Game (10-15 minutes)

  • vs Bio: Ling/Bane/Muta composition
  • vs Mech: Roach/Hydra with range
  • Overseers for cloaked units
  • Use speed for map control

Common Mistakes

  • Missing injects and losing larvae production
  • Poor creep spread limiting mobility
  • Over-droning vs aggression
  • Poor scouting of Terran's composition
  • Fighting off creep and losing speed advantage
  • Wrong unit choice vs Terran's army
  • No Overlord spread missing movements
  • Late tech falling behind in upgrades

Upgrade Priorities

Essential Early Upgrades

Must-Have Upgrades

  • Metabolic Boost: Zergling speed (essential)
  • Pneumatized Carapace: Overlord speed
  • Centrifugal Hooks: Baneling speed
  • Melee Attacks +1: Ling/Bane damage

Tech-Specific Upgrades

  • If Muta: Flyer Attacks, Flyer Carapace
  • If Roach/Hydra: Missile Attacks, Grooved Spines
  • Carapace: Always get armor upgrades
  • Advanced: Chitinous Plating, Muscular Augments

Advanced Techniques

Micro Techniques

  • Zergling Surrounds: Use speed to encircle Marines
  • Baneling Connections: Manual detonation timing
  • Muta Harassment: Hit and run vs Medivacs
  • Overlord Spreading: Map-wide vision network

Strategic Techniques

  • Creep Highways: Fast movement corridors
  • Multi-tasking: Harassment while main army fights
  • Base Trading: Use mobility for economic exchanges
  • Runby Threats: Constant pressure on Terran economy

Late Game Considerations

Hive Tech Timing

  • Hive Tech Timing: Around 15-18 minutes
  • Greater Spire: For Brood Lords vs ground
  • Ultralisk Cavern: For breakthrough units
  • Infestation Pit: For Vipers and Infestors
  • Multiple Evolutions: All upgrade paths

Practice Drills

  • Work on efficient Queen energy usage for creep
  • Perfect build order execution vs AI
  • Practice Ling/Bane vs Marine/Marauder micro
  • Work on multitasking inject while microing army
  • Practice information gathering efficiency

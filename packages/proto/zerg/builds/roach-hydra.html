Roach/Hydra Composition

Matchup: ZvP, ZvT

Difficulty: Intermediate

A powerful mid-to-late game army composition combining the tankiness of Roaches with the range and DPS of Hydralisks. This build excels against Protoss death balls and Terran mech compositions.

Build Order

  1. 9 Overlord
  2. 15 Hatchery
  3. 16 Spawning Pool
  4. 17 Extractor
  5. 18 Overlord
  6. @Pool: Queen, 4 Zerglings
  7. 22 Roach Warren
  8. @Natural: Queen
  9. 26 Lair
  10. 28 Extractor x2
  11. @Lair: Hydralisk Den
  12. 32 Evolution Chamber x2
  13. @Den: Hydralisk production
  14. Continue Roach/Hydra production
  15. Add Infestation Pit for Hive tech

Unit Composition

Army Ratios

Core Units

  • Roaches: 40-50% (front line tanks)
  • Hydralisks: 50-60% (DPS and anti-air)
  • Focus on range advantage
  • Excellent upgrade scaling

Support Units

  • Overseers for detection
  • Queens for transfuse
  • Infestors for crowd control
  • Vipers for late game support

Execution Guide

Early Game (0-8 minutes)

  • Standard economic opening
  • Get Roach Warren for early defense
  • Scout opponent's tech choices
  • Defend with Roaches if pressured
  • Focus on economic development

Tech Phase (8-12 minutes)

  • Get Lair as soon as economically safe
  • Add Hydralisk Den when Lair completes
  • Start Evolution Chambers for upgrades
  • Begin Hydralisk production
  • Take third base

Army Phase (12+ minutes)

  • Mass produce Roach/Hydra army
  • Get critical upgrades (+1/+1 minimum)
  • Control key map positions
  • Force favorable engagements
  • Tech to Hive for advanced units

Key Strategic Points

  • Range Advantage: Hydralisks outrange most units
  • Anti-Air: Strong against air compositions
  • Tanky Front-line: Roaches absorb damage
  • Upgrade Dependent: Scales excellently with upgrades
  • Positioning Critical: Army must be well-positioned

Unit Roles and Usage

Roach Role

Primary Functions

  • Tank damage in front line
  • Good vs armored units
  • Regeneration when out of combat
  • Fast movement for repositioning

Hydralisk Role

  • Primary damage dealers
  • Counter air units effectively
  • Attack from safe distance
  • Focus fire key targets

Positioning and Formation

Army Formation

  • Roaches Front: Tank damage in front line
  • Hydras Back: DPS from behind Roaches
  • Concave: Spread army in arc formation
  • High Ground: Use terrain advantages

Engagement Positioning

  • Range Advantage: Start fights at max Hydra range
  • Retreat Path: Always have escape route
  • Support Units: Keep Overseers/Queens safe
  • Flanking: Attack from multiple angles when possible

Matchup Specific Strategy

vs Protoss (ZvP)

Strengths

  • Excellent vs Gateway units, Colossi
  • Target priority: Colossi > Stalkers > Zealots
  • Range upgrade crucial vs Colossi
  • Add Vipers for Blinding Cloud

Watch For

  • High Templar and Psionic Storm
  • Archons with heavy splash damage
  • Void Rays and air transitions
  • Force Field usage by Sentries

vs Terran (ZvT)

Strengths

  • Excellent vs Mech, Battlecruisers
  • Target priority: Tanks > Thors > others
  • Use mobility to avoid Tank lines
  • Add Vipers vs mech, Infestors vs bio

Watch For

  • Bio compositions with stim
  • Liberator range and splash
  • Siege Tank defensive lines
  • Viking responses to air support

Economic Management

Gas Requirements

Resource Demands

  • High gas demand for both units
  • Take all geysers by 8 minutes
  • Gas priority: Den > Units > Upgrades
  • 6 drones on gas minimum

Base Management

  • Three base economy needed
  • 60-70 drones optimal
  • Critical inject timing
  • Third base around 8-10 minutes

Upgrade Priority

Essential Upgrades

  1. Grooved Spines: +1 Hydralisk range (critical)
  2. Missile Attacks +1: Roach and Hydra damage
  3. Carapace +1: Survivability for both units
  4. Muscular Augments: Hydralisk speed
  5. Tunneling Claws: Roach speed

Micro Techniques

Hydralisk Micro

Combat Micro

  • Focus fire single enemies
  • Use range to avoid damage
  • Spread against splash damage
  • Target threats first

Army Control

  • Maintain proper unit spacing
  • Pull back as single army
  • Rally new units to army
  • Always fight from good positions

Roach Micro

  • Tank Damage: Keep in front of Hydras
  • Regeneration: Pull back damaged Roaches
  • Flanking: Use speed for positioning
  • Target Selection: Focus armored units

Common Mistakes

  • Fighting without range upgrade vs Colossi
  • Poor army positioning and formation
  • Not getting enough gas for composition
  • Engaging in bad positions
  • Neglecting upgrades for more units
  • Clumping army against splash damage
  • Not using Roaches as proper tanks
  • Missing inject timings during battles

Advanced Techniques

Army Splitting

  • Multi-pronged Attacks: Attack from multiple angles
  • Flanking: Surround enemy army
  • Base Harassment: Small groups hit expansions
  • Retreat Splitting: Save units during retreat

Support Unit Integration

  • Overseers: Detection and vision
  • Infestors: Fungal Growth for crowd control
  • Vipers: Blinding Cloud vs ranged units
  • Queens: Transfuse for healing

Dealing with Counters

vs High Templar

Counter Strategies

  • Spread army to reduce Storm damage
  • Focus fire Templars first
  • Attack from multiple sides
  • Add Vipers for Abduct

vs Siege Tanks

  • Use mobility to avoid Tank lines
  • Attack from different directions
  • Vipers for Blinding Cloud
  • Focus fire Tanks quickly

Late Game Transitions

Hive Tech Additions

  • Lurkers: Add for area denial
  • Vipers: Support vs armored units
  • Brood Lords: Transition vs ground armies
  • Ultralisks: Add vs light units

Production Management

Building Requirements

Production Structures

  • Roach Warren: 1 per 2 bases minimum
  • Hydralisk Den: 1-2 depending on composition
  • Evolution Chambers: 2 for double upgrades
  • Hatcheries: 3-4 for proper production

Production Cycles

  • Always inject on time
  • Continuous unit production
  • Stay ahead on Overlords
  • Match gas income to production

Strengths and Weaknesses

Composition Analysis

Strengths

  • Excellent vs gateway units and Colossi
  • Strong anti-air capabilities
  • Good range and DPS
  • Scales well with upgrades

Weaknesses

  • Vulnerable to AoE damage
  • Slow army movement
  • High gas requirements
  • Positioning dependent

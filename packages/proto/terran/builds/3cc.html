Fast 3rd CC

Matchup: TvZ (primarily)

Difficulty: Intermediate-Advanced

This build is great against the TvZ matchup because the Zerg opponent will almost always be out-mining you. This economic strategy focuses on matching Zerg's expansion speed to compete in the late game.

Build Order

  1. 14 Supply Depot
  2. 16 Barracks
  3. 16 Refinery
  4. 19 Orbital Command
  5. 19 Supply Depot
  6. 21 Command Center (Natural)
  7. 21 Factory
  8. 23 Refinery
  9. 26 Starport
  10. 28 Command Center (Third)
  11. 30 Engineering Bay
  12. 32 Bunker (if needed vs pressure)
  13. @Factory: Hellions or Widow Mines
  14. @Starport: Medivacs or Vikings
  15. Continue economic development

Execution Guide

Early Game (0-6 minutes)

  • Execute standard Terran opening
  • Take natural expansion at 21 supply
  • Build Factory for tech structure
  • Scout Zerg's build order and aggression timing
  • Focus on SCV production over military

Economic Phase (6-10 minutes)

  • Take third Command Center early (around 6-7 minutes)
  • Build minimal defensive units (Hellions/Marines)
  • Get Starport for mobility/detection
  • Scout heavily for Zerg timing attacks
  • Prepare defensive measures if needed

Transition Phase (10+ minutes)

  • Saturate all three bases with SCVs
  • Add production facilities (Barracks/Factories)
  • Choose army composition based on Zerg's strategy
  • Get upgrades and tech up
  • Maintain economic advantage

Key Strategic Points

  • Economic Focus: Prioritize economy over early military
  • Scouting Critical: Must know when Zerg attacks
  • Defensive Preparation: Ready to defend timing attacks
  • Late Game Strength: Superior economy for extended games
  • Risk vs Reward: Vulnerable early, powerful late

Defending Against Zerg Aggression

Early Aggression (6-8 minutes)

Common Attacks

  • Speedling Floods: Wall off, bunkers, Marines
  • Roach Rushes: Marauders, bunkers, repair SCVs
  • Baneling Busts: Spread buildings, Marine splits

General Rule

  • Don't over-react to small attacks
  • Use minimum defense necessary
  • Keep economic focus

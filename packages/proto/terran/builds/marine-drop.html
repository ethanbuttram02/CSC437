Marine Drop Harassment

Matchup: All matchups

Difficulty: Intermediate

A harassment-focused build that uses Medivac drops to damage the enemy economy while building up at home. This build teaches multi-tasking and map control.

Build Order

  1. 14 Supply Depot
  2. 16 Barracks
  3. 16 Refinery
  4. 19 Orbital Command
  5. 19 Supply Depot
  6. 20 Marine
  7. 21 Factory
  8. 22 Barracks
  9. 24 Starport
  10. 25 Supply Depot
  11. @Starport: Medivac
  12. 27 Tech Lab on Barracks
  13. @Tech Lab: Stim Pack
  14. 30 Load 8 Marines in Medivac
  15. Continue Marine production

Execution Guide

Early Game (0-5 minutes)

  • Focus on clean build order execution
  • Scout opponent's main base around 3:30
  • Wall off your natural if needed
  • Keep producing SCVs constantly
  • Don't let production buildings sit idle

Mid Game (5-8 minutes)

  • Load Marines into Medivac as soon as it finishes
  • Target enemy worker lines first
  • Look for undefended expansions
  • Keep Medivac moving to avoid anti-air
  • Add more Barracks at home

Late Game Transition

  • Continue harassment with multiple Medivacs
  • Build up main army composition
  • Use drops as distraction for main attacks
  • Transition to Bio/Tank or Bio/Mine

Key Strategic Points

  • Target Priority: Workers > Tech buildings > Production
  • Positioning: Drop in mineral lines or behind enemy defenses
  • Micro: Stutter-step Marines to maximize damage
  • Retreat: Don't over-commit - preserve units for future drops
  • Multi-tasking: Keep producing at home while microing drops

Drop Techniques and Micro

Basic Drop Execution

Loading and Movement

  • Pre-spread Marines before dropping
  • Use boost to escape danger quickly
  • Vary flight paths to avoid interception
  • Drop on high ground when possible

Combat Micro

  • Stutter-step Marines for maximum DPS
  • Focus fire on important targets
  • Retreat when overwhelmed
  • Pick up Marines before they die

Advanced Drop Tactics

  • Multi-drop: Use multiple Medivacs simultaneously
  • Fake Drops: Threaten areas to force defenses
  • Coordination: Time drops with main army pushes
  • Reinforcement: Rally new Marines to drop zones
  • Map Control: Control watchtowers and key positions

Matchup Specific Strategy

vs Terran (TvT)

Targets & Tactics

  • Target SCVs repairing buildings
  • Hit undefended expansions
  • Avoid areas with Siege Tanks
  • Focus on production buildings

Watch For

  • Missile Turrets and Vikings
  • Marine/Tank defensive positions
  • Counter-drops from opponent
  • Follow-up: Add Vikings for air control

vs Protoss (TvP)

Targets & Tactics

  • Target Probes and tech buildings
  • Hit during Gateway transitions
  • Avoid Photon Cannon coverage
  • Focus on Pylons to cut power

Watch For

  • Stalkers and Phoenix responses
  • Photon Cannon defenses
  • Warp-in reinforcements
  • Follow-up: Add Vikings vs Phoenix

vs Zerg (TvZ)

Targets & Tactics

  • Target Drones and Overlords
  • Hit during drone-heavy phases
  • Avoid Queen positions
  • Focus on tech buildings (Spire, etc.)

Watch For

  • Queens and Spore Crawlers
  • Zergling counter-attacks
  • Mutalisk harassment response
  • Follow-up: Prepare for bio engagements

Common Mistakes

  • Flying Medivacs in straight lines (easy to intercept)
  • Not retreating when overwhelmed
  • Forgetting to produce at home during drops
  • Dropping in heavily defended areas
  • Not having follow-up drops ready
  • Poor Marine micro and stutter-stepping
  • Over-committing to unsuccessful drops
  • Not coordinating with main army movements

Economic Management

Production Balance

Home Economy

  • Continuous SCV production
  • Add Barracks for more Marines
  • Keep MULE production constant
  • Expand when drops are successful

Multi-tasking

  • Macro while microing drops
  • Queue Marines during battles
  • Monitor supply and production
  • Don't neglect base development

Transition Options

If Drops Succeed

  • Continue Pressure: Add more Medivacs for constant harassment
  • Economic Advantage: Expand behind successful drops
  • Tech Up: Add Vikings, Tanks, or advanced tech
  • Army Build-up: Focus on main army while opponent recovers

If Drops Fail

  • Defensive Mode: Use Marines for home defense
  • Different Strategy: Switch to different harassment or macro
  • Army Focus: Build up ground army instead
  • Economic Development: Focus on expansion and tech

Advanced Multi-tasking

  • Dual Control: Micro drops while managing main army
  • Production Cycles: Queue units during micro-intensive moments
  • Map Awareness: Track enemy army movements
  • Resource Management: Monitor resources during battles
  • Supply Management: Build Supply Depots proactively

Upgrade Priority

  1. Stim Pack: Essential for Marine effectiveness
  2. Combat Shield: Marine survivability
  3. +1 Attack: From Engineering Bay
  4. Medivac Upgrade: Enhanced boost or healing
  5. +1 Armor: After attack upgrade

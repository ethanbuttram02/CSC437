Banshee Rush

Matchup: TvT, TvP

Difficulty: Intermediate

A tech rush that can end games quickly against unprepared opponents. This high-risk, high-reward strategy focuses on getting cloaked Banshees as fast as possible to devastate the enemy economy.

Build Order

  1. 14 Supply Depot
  2. 16 Barracks
  3. 16 Refinery
  4. 19 Orbital Command
  5. 19 Supply Depot
  6. 20 Marine
  7. 21 Factory
  8. 22 Refinery
  9. 24 Starport
  10. 25 Supply Depot
  11. @Starport: Tech Lab
  12. @Tech Lab: Cloak Research
  13. @Cloak: Banshee production
  14. Continue Banshee production
  15. Add more Starports if successful

Execution Guide

Early Game (0-4 minutes)

  • Focus on clean build order execution
  • Hide your tech choice from enemy scouts
  • Build Starport in a concealed location if possible
  • Maintain constant SCV production
  • Use Marine for basic defense only

Tech Phase (4-6 minutes)

  • Research Cloak immediately when Tech Lab finishes
  • Start first Banshee as soon as Cloak begins
  • Scout enemy base for detection
  • Prepare for early defense if needed

Attack Phase (6+ minutes)

  • Attack with first Banshee as soon as Cloak finishes
  • Target SCVs/Probes first, then key buildings
  • Retreat immediately if enemy has detection
  • Continuously produce more Banshees
  • Consider adding more Starports if doing damage

Key Strategic Points

  • Stealth is Key: Hide your Starport from enemy scouts
  • Target Priority: Workers > Production > Tech buildings
  • Micro: Keep Banshees moving to avoid being caught
  • Detection Check: Always scout for Turrets/Observers/Overseers
  • All-in Commitment: Don't half-commit once you start

Matchup Specific Strategy

vs Terran

Strategy

  • Target SCVs repairing buildings
  • Hit multiple bases
  • Focus on production facilities

Watch For

  • Engineering Bays and Turrets
  • Vikings and Marines
  • Avoid areas with Siege Tanks

vs Protoss

Strategy

  • Target Probes and Pylons
  • Hit before Robotics Facility completes
  • Focus on tech buildings

Watch For

  • Observers and Photon Cannons
  • Stalkers and Phoenix
  • Robotics Facility timing

vs Zerg (Not Recommended)

Why Avoid

  • Zerg gets Overseers easily
  • Queens provide strong anti-air
  • Spore Crawlers shut down Banshees

Alternative

  • Consider other builds vs Zerg
  • Bio or Mech compositions

Micro Techniques

  • Hit and Run: Attack, then move to avoid retaliation
  • Worker Focus: Always prioritize killing workers
  • Building Targeting: Destroy key production structures
  • Escape Routes: Always have an exit strategy
  • Multiple Angles: Attack from different directions

Common Mistakes

  • Revealing Starport too early to enemy scouts
  • Not scouting for enemy detection
  • Attacking before Cloak finishes
  • Over-committing against prepared opponents
  • Poor Banshee micro - getting caught by anti-air
  • Not having a backup plan if rush fails

Advanced Tips

  • Build Starport away from main base to avoid detection
  • Use SCVs to scout for detection before attacking
  • Consider researching Hyperflight Rotors for speed
  • Attack multiple bases simultaneously if you have enough Banshees
  • Use Banshees to deny opponent's expansion attempts

Transition Options

If Rush Succeeds

  • Continue Banshee production
  • Add more Starports
  • Transition to normal army composition
  • Take expansion behind the pressure

If Rush Fails

  • Transition to Bio or Mech
  • Focus on economy and defense
  • Use Banshees for harassment only
  • Prepare for opponent's counter-attack

Counters and How to Avoid Them

Common Counters

  • Missile Turrets: Scout and avoid, or attack elsewhere
  • Mobile Detection: Focus fire the detector first
  • Mass Anti-Air: Retreat and transition builds
  • Early Rush: Have minimal defense ready

4-Gate Rush

Matchup: All matchups (especially PvP)

Difficulty: Beginner

A classic aggressive build that focuses on overwhelming the opponent with early gateway units. Perfect for learning Protoss fundamentals and warp-in mechanics.

Back to Protoss Builds

Build Order

  1. 9 Pylon
  2. 13 Gateway
  3. 15 Assimilator
  4. 16 Pylon
  5. 18 Cybernetics Core
  6. 19 Zealot (Chronoboost)
  7. 20 Warpgate Research (Chronoboost)
  8. 22 Pylon
  9. 23 Stalker
  10. 24 Gateway x3
  11. @Warpgate: Transform all Gateways
  12. Continuous unit production via warp-ins

Execution Guide

Opening (0-4 minutes)

  • Probe scout at 9 supply
  • Chronoboost first Zealot for early pressure
  • Chronoboost Warpgate research (most important)
  • Keep producing Probes constantly

Mid Game (4-6 minutes)

  • Build all 4 Gateways simultaneously
  • Send a Probe forward for proxy pylon
  • Transform to Warpgates when research completes
  • Warp in 4 units immediately

Attack Phase (6+ minutes)

  • Attack with first wave of units
  • Continuously warp in reinforcements
  • Focus on overwhelming enemy defenses
  • Target workers and production buildings

Key Strategic Points

  • Chronoboost Priority: Warpgate research > Unit production > Probes
  • Proxy Pylon: Essential for forward reinforcements
  • Unit Mix: Start with Zealots, add Stalkers vs ranged units
  • Timing: Attack when Warpgate completes (~6:00)
  • Reinforcement: Warp in units every 32 seconds

Unit Composition

vs Terran

  • Zealot/Stalker mix (3:1 ratio)
  • Stalkers for Marauders and Bunkers
  • Zealots for Marines and SCVs

vs Protoss

  • Heavy Stalker focus
  • Zealots only if opponent goes heavy Zealot
  • Micro Stalkers to kite Zealots

vs Zerg

  • Stalker/Zealot mix
  • Zealots for Zerglings
  • Stalkers for Roaches and Spine Crawlers

Common Mistakes

  • Not chronoboosting Warpgate research
  • Forgetting to bring forward Probe
  • Building Gateways too early
  • Wrong unit composition for matchup
  • Not continuing warp-ins during battle

Advanced Techniques

  • Multiple proxy pylons for different attack paths
  • Fake retreat to draw enemy out of position
  • Focus fire on key enemy units
  • Use Zealot charge to tank damage
  • Warp in reinforcements behind enemy lines

Defending Against 4-Gate

  • Scout for proxy pylons
  • Build defensive structures early
  • Get detection vs Dark Templar follow-up
  • Counter-attack if enemy over-commits

Transition Options

  • If successful: Expand and tech up
  • If unsuccessful: Defend and transition to Robo/Stargate
  • Economic: Take natural expansion
  • Tech: Add Robotics Facility or Stargate

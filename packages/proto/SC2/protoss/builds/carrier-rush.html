Carrier Rush

Matchup: PvT, PvZ

Difficulty: Advanced

A late-game oriented strategy that rushes to Carriers for overwhelming air superiority. This build requires excellent defensive play early while teching up to the most powerful air units in the game.

Execution Guide

Early Game (0-8 minutes)

  • Focus on economy and survival
  • Build minimal gateway units for defense
  • Take natural expansion safely
  • Scout heavily for enemy aggression
  • Get first Stargate for defensive air units

Tech Phase (8-12 minutes)

  • Build Fleet Beacon for Carrier tech
  • Add more Stargates (2-3 total)
  • Defend with Phoenix/Void Rays
  • Take third base when safe
  • Focus on surviving enemy timing attacks

Carrier Phase (12+ minutes)

  • Mass produce Carriers
  • Build up to 6-8 Carriers
  • Add support units (Phoenix, Archons)
  • Attack when Carrier mass is ready
  • Use Carrier range advantage

Key Strategic Points

  • Survival: Must survive until Carriers are ready
  • Economy: Need strong economic foundation
  • Patience: Don't attack too early
  • Mass: Carriers are strongest in groups
  • Support: Need other units to protect Carriers

Carrier Mechanics

Interceptor Management

  • Build Interceptors: Always keep Carriers fully loaded
  • Interceptor Count: 8 interceptors per Carrier
  • Auto-rebuild: Interceptors rebuild automatically
  • Range: Interceptors have 8 range attack
  • Micro: Pull back damaged Carriers

Carrier Positioning

  • Back Line: Keep Carriers behind support units
  • Spread: Don't clump against splash damage
  • Range Usage: Attack from maximum range
  • Retreat Path: Always have escape route
  • High Ground: Use terrain advantages

Defense During Tech-Up

Early Defense Units

  • Phoenix: vs air harassment and light units
  • Void Rays: vs ground armies and buildings
  • Stalkers: Anti-air and general defense
  • Zealots: Meat shield and mineral dump
  • Cannons: Static defense at key locations

Defensive Positioning

  • Wall off natural with buildings
  • Place Cannons at key choke points
  • Keep air units mobile for defense
  • Use Force Fields to buy time
  • Retreat to main base if overwhelmed

Matchup Specific Strategy

vs Terran

  • Threats: Bio drops, Viking mass, Battlecruisers
  • Defense: Phoenix for drops, Stalkers for Vikings
  • Support Units: High Templar for EMP protection
  • Timing: Attack before BC/Viking mass
  • Target Priority: Production, then army

vs Zerg

  • Threats: Corruptor/Hydra, Muta harassment
  • Defense: Phoenix for Mutas, Archons for Corruptors
  • Support Units: Storm for clumped anti-air
  • Timing: Before massive Corruptor count
  • Target Priority: Hatcheries, then army

Army Composition

Core Army

  • Carriers: 6-8 for main force
  • Phoenix: 4-6 for air superiority
  • Archons: 2-4 for splash damage
  • High Templar: 2-3 for Storm support

Support Units

  • Stalkers: Anti-air support
  • Void Rays: vs heavy armor
  • Mothership: Late-game support

Common Timing Attacks to Defend

6-8 Minute Attacks

  • Bio Stim Timing: Use Phoenix and Cannons
  • Roach Rush: Void Rays and Force Fields
  • 4-Gate: Defensive Stalkers and Cannons

10-12 Minute Attacks

  • 2-Base All-ins: Full defensive setup
  • Tank Pushes: Air units and mobility
  • Hydra/Roach: Void Rays and splash damage

Economic Management

  • Base Saturation: Keep all bases fully saturated
  • Gas Priority: Carriers are very gas-heavy
  • Expansion Timing: Third base around 8-10 minutes
  • Chronoboost Usage: Carriers and Probes
  • Production: 3-4 Stargates for Carrier production

Common Mistakes

  • Attacking too early with insufficient Carriers
  • Not building enough interceptors
  • Poor Carrier positioning vs anti-air
  • Neglecting economic development
  • Not defending adequately during tech-up
  • Forgetting support units for Carriers
  • Over-committing Carriers in bad fights

Upgrade Priority

  1. Warpgate: For gateway unit production
  2. Air Weapons +1: Increases Carrier damage
  3. Air Armor +1: Carrier survivability
  4. Graviton Catapult: Increased interceptor range
  5. Psionic Storm: If using High Templar

Advanced Techniques

  • Carrier Micro: Pull back damaged Carriers
  • Interceptor Splitting: Reduce splash damage
  • Multi-angle Attack: Attack from different sides
  • Support Coordination: Use Storm and other abilities
  • Base Trading: Use mobility advantage

Dealing with Counters

vs Mass Anti-Air

  • Add more Phoenix for air superiority
  • Use High Templar for Storm
  • Better Carrier positioning
  • Attack from multiple angles

vs Early Aggression

  • Sacrifice economy for defense if needed
  • Use Cannons and gateway units
  • Delay Carrier timing if necessary
  • Consider transitioning builds

Transition Options

  • Air Army: Add Void Rays and Phoenix
  • Ground Support: Add Zealot/Archon/HT
  • Economic Focus: Take more bases
  • Mothership: Late-game support unit

Practice Drills

  • Practice surviving early timing attacks
  • Work on Carrier micro vs anti-air
  • Practice economic management
  • Learn optimal defensive setups
  • Practice transitioning if rush fails

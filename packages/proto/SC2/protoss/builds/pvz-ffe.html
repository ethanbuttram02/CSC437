PvZ Forge Fast Expand

Matchup: Protoss vs Zerg

Difficulty: Advanced

An economic build that uses static defense to fast expand while staying safe against Zerg aggression. This build prioritizes early upgrades and economic advantage for strong late-game potential.

Execution Guide

Opening (0-4 minutes)

  • Scout at 9 with Probe to check Zerg's opening
  • Build Forge before Nexus for safety
  • Place Nexus at natural expansion
  • Wall off with Gateway and Cannons

Defense Phase (4-8 minutes)

  • Build 2-3 Photon Cannons at natural
  • Start +1 Attack upgrade immediately
  • Add Gateway and Cybernetics Core
  • Scout for Zerg's tech choices
  • Maintain constant Probe production

Tech Transition (8-12 minutes)

  • Add 2 more Gateways
  • Build Robotics Facility
  • Start unit production (Zealots/Stalkers)
  • Get Observer for scouting
  • Prepare for Zerg's mid-game timing

Defensive Setup

Wall Construction

  • Gateway: Part of the wall
  • Pylon: Powers Cannons and completes wall
  • Cannons: 2-3 at natural choke
  • Probe Gap: Leave small gap for worker movement

Cannon Placement

  • Choke Coverage: Cover main approach
  • Overlapping Fields: Mutual cannon support
  • Nexus Protection: At least one cannon near Nexus
  • High Ground: Use terrain advantages

Key Strategic Points

  • Early Economy: Fast second base for economic lead
  • Safe Defense: Cannons hold off early aggression
  • Upgrade Advantage: +1 Attack timing is crucial
  • Scouting: Observer for Zerg tech information
  • Scaling: Strong late-game potential

Scouting Information

What to Look For

  • Zergling Rush: Early Pool and no expansion
  • Baneling Bust: Baneling Nest timing
  • Roach Rush: Roach Warren and timing
  • Fast Spire: Early air harassment
  • Economic Play: Fast third base

Responses to Scout

  • vs Zergling Rush: More Cannons, delay tech
  • vs Baneling Bust: Extra Cannons, Force Fields
  • vs Roach Rush: Immortals, more units
  • vs Mutalisk: Phoenix, Stalkers
  • vs Economic: Standard tech progression

Common Zerg Attacks and Defenses

Zergling Rush

  • Defense: 2-3 Cannons, wall completion
  • Response: Hold position, don't chase
  • Follow-up: Counter-attack when safe

Baneling Bust

  • Defense: Force Fields, Cannon spacing
  • Response: Repair buildings, focus Banelings
  • Follow-up: Rebuild wall if damaged

Roach Push

  • Defense: Immortals, Stalkers, Force Fields
  • Response: Focus fire Roaches
  • Follow-up: Counter-attack with upgraded units

Tech Transitions

Standard Transition

  • Add Robotics Facility after Gateways
  • Get Observer for scouting
  • Tech to Immortals or Colossi
  • Continue gateway unit production

vs Air Harassment

  • Add Stargate for Phoenix
  • Build Stalkers for anti-air
  • Consider Photon Cannons at mineral lines

vs Heavy Ground

  • Focus on Colossus tech
  • Add High Templar for Psionic Storm
  • Get Charge for Zealots

Common Mistakes

  • Poor Cannon placement - not covering approaches
  • Not scouting Zerg's tech choices
  • Delaying +1 Attack upgrade
  • Over-investing in static defense
  • Not transitioning to units fast enough
  • Forgetting to build Probes during pressure
  • Poor wall construction leaving gaps

Upgrade Priority

  1. +1 Attack: Essential early upgrade
  2. Warpgate: For unit production
  3. +1 Armor: After attack upgrade
  4. Charge: For Zealot effectiveness
  5. Extended Thermal Lance: If going Colossus

Advanced Techniques

  • Cannon Rushing: Offensive Cannons in Zerg base
  • Hidden Tech: Proxy Robotics/Stargate
  • Economic Harassment: Zealot runbys
  • Timing Attacks: +1 Attack timing push
  • Multi-base Play: Fast third base behind defense

Economic Management

  • Probe Production: Constant worker creation
  • Chronoboost Usage: Probes, then upgrades
  • Base Saturation: 16 Probes per mineral line
  • Gas Timing: Take gas when needed for tech
  • Third Base: Around 10-12 minutes if safe

Late Game Transitions

  • Deathball: Colossus/High Templar/Gateway
  • Air Transition: Carriers/Void Rays
  • Harassment: Warp Prism/DT drops
  • Turtle Style: Defensive with superior upgrades

Dealing with Specific Units

vs Mutalisks

  • Build Phoenix from Stargate
  • Add Stalkers for ground-based anti-air
  • Photon Cannons at key locations

vs Lurkers

  • Get Observer for detection
  • Use Immortals and Colossi
  • Avoid choke points

vs Brood Lords

  • Mass Phoenix or Void Rays
  • High Templar for Psionic Storm
  • Avoid direct engagement

Practice Drills

  • Learn optimal Probe rally points
  • Practice smooth tech transitions

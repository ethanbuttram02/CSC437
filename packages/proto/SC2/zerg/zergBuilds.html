Build Overviews

ZvZ Speedling Rush

Matchup: Zerg vs Zerg | Difficulty: Beginner

Advantages:

  • Fast and aggressive opening
  • Can end games quickly
  • Good vs greedy Zerg openings
  • Teaches early game mechanics
  • Forces opponent to respond defensively

Disadvantages:

  • All-in nature if unsuccessful
  • Vulnerable to defensive builds
  • Requires good micro
  • Limited economic development
  • Can fall behind if countered

ZvT Macro Hatch

Matchup: Zerg vs Terran | Difficulty: Intermediate

Advantages:

  • Strong economic foundation
  • Excellent drone production
  • Good vs Terran macro builds
  • Flexible tech options
  • Strong late-game potential

Disadvantages:

  • Vulnerable to early aggression
  • Requires excellent scouting
  • Delayed military units
  • Can be punished by timing attacks
  • Needs good inject timing

ZvP Roach Rush

Matchup: Zerg vs Protoss | Difficulty: Beginner

Advantages:

  • Strong vs gateway units
  • Good timing attack
  • Tanky units with good HP
  • Can break force fields
  • Effective vs Protoss expansions

Disadvantages:

  • Weak vs Immortals and Colossi
  • Can be kited by Stalkers
  • Limited vs air units
  • Falls off in late game
  • Vulnerable to defensive play

12 Pool Opening

Matchup: All matchups | Difficulty: Beginner

Advantages:

  • Safe and reliable opening
  • Good vs most early aggression
  • Allows for early defense
  • Flexible follow-up options
  • Standard opening for learning

Disadvantages:

  • Slightly delayed economy vs hatch first
  • Less aggressive than early pool builds
  • Can be out-economied by greedier builds
  • Requires good transitions

Ling/Bane/Muta

Matchup: ZvT, ZvZ | Difficulty: Advanced

Advantages:

  • Extremely mobile army
  • Excellent harassment potential
  • Good vs bio compositions
  • Strong map control
  • Multi-pronged attack capability

Disadvantages:

  • Very micro-intensive
  • Vulnerable to splash damage
  • Weak vs heavy armor
  • Requires excellent multitasking
  • Gas-heavy composition

Roach/Hydra Composition

Matchup: ZvP, ZvT | Difficulty: Intermediate

Advantages:

  • Good range and DPS
  • Strong vs air units
  • Tanky front-line with Roaches
  • Good vs Protoss death balls
  • Scales well with upgrades

Disadvantages:

  • Slow army movement
  • Vulnerable to splash damage
  • Expensive army composition
  • Can be outmaneuvered
  • Weak vs heavy AOE units

General Zerg Strategy Tips

  • Inject Larvae: Always keep your Queens injecting for constant unit production
  • Creep Spread: Use Queens to spread creep for map control and faster unit movement
  • Scouting: Use Overlords and Zerglings to maintain vision of enemy movements
  • Macro: Zerg excels at economy - prioritize drone production when safe
  • Unit Composition: Adapt your army based on what your opponent is building

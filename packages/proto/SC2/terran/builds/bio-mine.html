Bio-Mine Composition

Matchup: TvZ, TvP

Difficulty: Advanced

A mobile army composition combining Marines/Marauders with Widow Mines for area denial and burst damage. This build excels at controlling space and punishing enemy positioning mistakes.

Unit Composition

Core Army

  • Marines: 60-70% of army supply
  • Marauders: 20-25% (vs Protoss), 10-15% (vs Zerg)
  • Widow Mines: 8-12 mines per army
  • Medivacs: 1 per 16 bio units

Support Units

  • Ghosts: vs High Templar/Infestors
  • Vikings: vs Colossi/Brood Lords
  • Liberators: For siege support

Execution Guide

Early Game (0-6 minutes)

  • Execute standard bio opening
  • Add Factory after second Barracks
  • Start Widow Mine production immediately
  • Get first 2-4 Mines before major engagements

Mid Game (6-12 minutes)

  • Research Drilling Claws upgrade
  • Position Mines in key choke points
  • Use Bio force to control enemy movement
  • Constantly reinforce Mine positions
  • Begin harassment with Medivac drops

Late Game (12+ minutes)

  • Maintain 15-20 Mines across the map
  • Use Mines for base defense
  • Control key areas with Mine fields
  • Support with appropriate counter units

Widow Mine Usage

Positioning

  • Choke Points: Block enemy movement
  • High Ground: Increase vision and safety
  • Resource Lines: Protect expansions
  • Retreat Paths: Cover your army's escape routes
  • Attack Routes: Support your pushes

Micro Techniques

  • Burrow Timing: Hide before enemy arrives
  • Spacing: Don't clump Mines together
  • Targeting: Focus high-value targets
  • Re-positioning: Move Mines as battle evolves
  • Friendly Fire Avoidance: Keep Bio units away

Key Strategic Points

  • Area Denial: Control key map areas with Mine fields
  • Mobility: Use Medivacs to reposition quickly
  • Upgrades: Prioritize weapon upgrades for Bio units
  • Map Control: Use Mines to secure territory
  • Cost Efficiency: Mines provide excellent bang for buck

Matchup Specific Strategy

vs Zerg

  • Target: Zerglings, Banelings, Hydralisks
  • Positioning: Cover creep highways
  • Timing: Hit before Hive tech units
  • Support: Add Liberators vs Lurkers
  • Micro: Avoid Baneling connections

vs Protoss

  • Target: Zealots, Stalkers, gateway units
  • Positioning: Block warp-in locations
  • Timing: Before Colossus/High Templar mass
  • Support: Add Vikings vs Colossi
  • Micro: Spread vs AoE damage

Common Mistakes

  • Clumping Widow Mines together
  • Not getting Drilling Claws upgrade
  • Poor Mine positioning in fights
  • Friendly fire from your own Mines
  • Not replacing destroyed Mines
  • Over-committing to Mine production
  • Forgetting to burrow Mines

Advanced Techniques

  • Mine Drops: Use Medivacs to place Mines behind enemy lines
  • Defensive Fields: Create Mine fields around bases
  • Leapfrog Positioning: Move Mines forward as you advance
  • Decoy Mines: Use visible Mines to control enemy movement
  • Multi-pronged Attacks: Coordinate Bio and Mine positioning

Upgrades Priority

  1. Stim Pack: Essential for Bio mobility
  2. Combat Shield: Increases Marine survivability
  3. Drilling Claws: Faster Mine burrow time
  4. +1 Attack: Increases overall army damage
  5. Concussive Shells: If using many Marauders
  6. +1 Armor: Reduces incoming damage

Counters and Solutions

Enemy Counters

  • Detection: Observers, Overseers, Scans
  • Long Range: Colossi, Siege Tanks
  • Air Units: Mutalisk, Phoenix harassment
  • AoE Damage: Banelings, High Templar

Your Solutions

  • vs Detection: Focus fire detectors first
  • vs Long Range: Add Vikings/Ghosts
  • vs Air: Marines provide good anti-air
  • vs AoE: Better unit spreading and positioning

Transition Options

  • Late Game Bio: Add Ghosts and Liberators
  • Mech Transition: Add Tanks and Thors
  • Sky Terran: Transition to Battlecruisers
  • Pure Bio: Phase out Mines for more Bio units

Practice Drills

  • Practice Mine placement in different maps
  • Work on avoiding friendly fire in battles
  • Practice Medivac + Mine drops
  • Learn optimal Mine spacing patterns
  • Practice quick Mine repositioning

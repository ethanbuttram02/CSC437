3-Rax With 1-1-1 Stim Timing

Matchup: All matchups

Difficulty: Beginner-Intermediate

This is a great all-arounder and very safe build in lower leagues because of how many units you will have early on. Perfect for learning Terran fundamentals and timing attacks.

Back to Terran Builds

Build Order

  1. 14 Supply Depot
  2. 16 Barracks
  3. 16 Refinery
  4. 19 Orbital Command
  5. 19 Supply Depot
  6. 20 Marine
  7. 21 Reactor (on Barracks)
  8. 21 Command Center
  9. 22 Supply Depot
  10. 24 Barracks x2
  11. 26 Tech Lab (on one Barracks)
  12. 28 Factory
  13. @Tech Lab: Stim Pack, Combat Shield
  14. 30 Engineering Bay
  15. @Factory: Reactor, then Starport
  16. @Starport: Medivac
  17. Attack with Stim timing (~6:30)

Execution Guide

Opening (0-4 minutes)

  • Build Supply Depot at 17-18 second mark
  • Wall off with Barracks and Supply Depot
  • Use SCV that built Barracks for scouting
  • Get Refinery and saturate with 3 SCVs
  • Constant SCV production

Production Phase (4-6 minutes)

  • Add Reactor to first Barracks for double Marine production
  • Build Command Center at natural expansion
  • Add 2 more Barracks (total of 3)
  • Put Tech Lab on one Barracks for upgrades
  • Start Stim Pack and Combat Shield research
  • Add Factory for 1-1-1 tech structure

Attack Phase (6-7 minutes)

  • Attack when Stim Pack completes
  • Bring 12-16 Marines with Stim
  • Add Starport and get Medivacs for follow-up
  • Continue Marine production during attack
  • Use micro to maximize Marine effectiveness

Key Strategic Points

  • Wall-off: Essential vs early rushes
  • Production Cycle: Never let Barracks sit idle
  • Timing: Attack as soon as Stim completes
  • Micro: Stutter-step Marines for maximum DPS
  • Follow-up: Add Medivacs for sustainability

Detailed Execution Notes

Wall Construction

  • Use Engineering Bay to line up the 3x3 Barracks for proper wall
  • Leave small gap for SCV movement
  • Against Zerg, wall off natural with Supply Depot
  • Consider Bunker at natural vs Zerg cheese

Gas Management

  • Take first Refinery after Barracks
  • Saturate with 3 SCVs immediately
  • Add 2 more Refineries around 6-7 minutes
  • Gas is needed for Stim, upgrades, and Factory tech

Production Management

  • Reactor Barracks: Constant Marine production
  • Tech Lab Barracks: Research upgrades, then Marauders
  • Third Barracks: Add Tech Lab later or keep making Marines
  • Factory: Reactor for Hellions, or swap for Starport
  • Starport: Medivacs for Marine support

Matchup Specific Strategy

vs Terran (TvT)

  • Focus: Marine/Marauder mix
  • Targets: SCVs and production buildings
  • Watch For: Bunkers and Tank support
  • Micro: Stutter-step vs Marines
  • Follow-up: Add Tanks for positional play

vs Protoss (TvP)

  • Focus: Marine/Marauder (4-6 Marauders)
  • Targets: Probes and tech buildings
  • Watch For: Force Fields and Stalkers
  • Upgrade: Get Concussive Shells for Marauders
  • Follow-up: Vikings vs Colossi

vs Zerg (TvZ)

  • Focus: Pure Marines or Marine-heavy
  • Targets: Drones and Queens
  • Watch For: Banelings and Spine Crawlers
  • Positioning: Fight away from creep
  • Follow-up: Hellions for harassment

Marine Micro Techniques

Stutter-Step

  • Technique: Attack, move, attack, move
  • Purpose: Maximize damage while staying mobile
  • Timing: Move right after attack animation
  • Practice: Essential skill for bio play

Focus Fire

  • Targets: Focus on single enemy units
  • Priority: Threats first (Stalkers, Marauders)
  • Method: Select all Marines, right-click target
  • Efficiency: Reduces enemy DPS faster

Splitting

  • vs Banelings: Spread Marines to reduce splash
  • vs AoE: Don't clump against Colossi/Tanks
  • Method: Select groups and move separately
  • Practice: Use split hotkeys for speed

Common Mistakes

  • Building Supply Depot too late (supply block)
  • Not walling off vs early aggression
  • Forgetting to research Stim Pack
  • Attacking before Stim completes
  • Poor Marine micro during engagements
  • Not following up with Medivacs
  • Stopping SCV production during attack
  • Not adapting unit composition to matchup

Advanced Tips

  • Chronology: Time your upgrades with attack timing
  • Multi-tasking: Continue production while microing
  • Scouting: Use first Marine or SCV to scout
  • Expansion: Take natural behind successful attack
  • Transitions: Adapt based on damage dealt

Economic Considerations

  • SCV Count: ~24 SCVs when attacking
  • MULE Usage: Drop MULEs constantly
  • Natural Timing: Orbital at natural around 5:00
  • Saturation: Don't over-saturate early

Upgrade Priority

  1. Stim Pack: Essential for timing attack
  2. Combat Shield: Marine survivability
  3. +1 Attack: From Engineering Bay
  4. Concussive Shells: If using Marauders vs Protoss
  5. +1 Armor: After attack upgrade

Transition Options

If Attack Succeeds

  • Continue pressure with reinforcements
  • Expand and add more production
  • Tech to Medivacs for sustained pressure
  • Add appropriate counter units

If Attack Fails

  • Retreat and defend
  • Focus on economy and upgrades
  • Transition to different army composition
  • Prepare for opponent's counter-attack

Practice Recommendations

  • Build Order: Practice vs Easy AI until perfect
  • Marine Micro: Stutter-step in Unit Tester
  • Multitasking: Practice production while microing
  • Timing: Hit attack timing consistently
  • Adaptation: Practice vs different races
